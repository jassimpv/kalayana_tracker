Kerala Wedding Expense Planner Guide

Kerala weddings have their own cost structure compared to a typical North Indian wedding — auditorium bookings, sadhya catering by the plate, and gold jewellery often make up a larger share of the budget. Here's how to plan a Kerala wedding budget planner around these specifics.

Auditorium & hall booking

Most Kerala weddings book a community auditorium or hall, often with separate charges for the hall, air-conditioning, and generator backup. Confirm what's included before treating the quoted price as final.

Sadhya & catering

Catering is usually quoted per plate for the sadhya, with extra charges for any additional items beyond the standard menu. Multiply the per-plate rate by your expected guest count, plus a buffer of 10–15% for walk-ins.

Gold & jewellery

Jewellery is typically one of the largest line items in a Kerala wedding budget. Track the gold rate on the day of purchase alongside making charges, since both affect the final cost significantly.

Photography & mehndi

Candid photography and videography packages, along with bridal mehndi, are usually booked separately from the main catering and venue vendor.

Putting it together
